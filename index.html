Placement test for vocabulary, verbs, and comprehension.

Progress tracking and skill breakdowns.

Learning Objectives

  • Learn 100 core everyday words.
  • Master present tense conjugations of regular verbs.
  • Understand gender and number agreement.
  • Hold a basic 3–5 sentence dialogue.
  • Read short passages and identify main ideas.

Worksheets

Printable and interactive practice materials: conjugation drills, vocabulary matching, short reading tasks.

Quizzes & Exams

Sample Quiz (Greetings)

  1. Choose the correct translation for "Good morning": Buenos días.
  2. Fill in: "Me llamo ___." → Juan.
  3. Match: Adiós → Goodbye; Gracias → Thank you.

Exam structure: Vocabulary (20), Grammar (30), Reading (25), Listening (25).
